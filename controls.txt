Movement Controls:  
W - Move forward (into the screen)  
S - Move backward (away from the screen)  
A - Move left  
D - Move right  
Space - Move upward  
Ctrl - Move downward  

Camera Controls:  
Left Click + Drag - Rotate the view 

Simulation Controls:  
Q - Start the simulation  
E - Stop the simulation  
R - Reset the simulation 
F - Toggle Fullscreen Mode
